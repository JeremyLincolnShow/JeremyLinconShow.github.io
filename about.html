About The Show

Conversations over craft. Stories behind the brew.

Welcome to The Jeremy Lincoln Show

Ever wonder what separates a garage homebrewer from a professional craft brewery owner? I'm Jeremy Lincoln, a passionate homebrewer, DIYer, and storyteller who loves exploring the world of craft beer and the people who make it happen.

Each episode dives into homebrewing tips, craft beer stories, and interviews with brewers, makers, and beer enthusiasts chasing their passion. Whether you're brewing in your garage, running a small brewery, or just love hearing how great beer comes to life, you'll find something here to inspire you.

Topics We Explore:

Homebrewing techniques
Professional brewing
Equipment reviews
Brewing science
Hop farming & malt production
Craft beer business
Recipe development
Beer styles & sensory evaluation
Community & clubs

Whether you're brewing your first batch, running a brewery, or just love craft beer stories - you'll find your people here. Pull up a chair, crack a cold one, and subscribe for weekly episodes.
